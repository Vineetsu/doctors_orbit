Frequently Asked Questions

Is the MBBS degree from Russia valid in India?

Yes, absolutely. The degrees from our partner universities are recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC), World Health Organization (WHO), and other major medical councils worldwide. After completion, you need to clear the FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduates Examination) to practice in India.

What is the medium of instruction?

The entire MBBS course is taught in English. However, Russian language classes are also provided to help students communicate with patients during clinical practice.

What is the total cost for the complete MBBS course?

The total cost for 6 years of MBBS in Russia ranges between 20-30 lakhs INR, which includes tuition fees, hostel accommodation, and other expenses. This is significantly lower than private medical colleges in India.

Is there any age limit for applying?

Students must be at least 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year. There is no upper age limit for pursuing MBBS in Russia.

Do I need to qualify NEET for MBBS in Russia?

Yes, qualifying NEET is mandatory for Indian students who wish to study MBBS abroad. However, the cut-off scores for Russian universities are much lower compared to Indian medical colleges.
